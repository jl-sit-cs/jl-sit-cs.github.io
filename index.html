Project Overview

Glasgow City Council (GCC) is committed to its Biodiversity Action Plan, requiring accurate and regular data on plant and wildlife species across the city. The Glasgow Botanic Gardens, a key site, hosts both cultivated and native species. Currently, species identification and data entry are largely manual, leading to inefficiencies.

This project aims to streamline the process of recording biological sightings, reduce manual data entry, and expand citizen participation through the iNaturalist app, ensuring continuous data collection year-round.
