About Our Project

Our Mission

We are dedicated to enhancing species recording by reducing manual data entry and leveraging citizen science tools like iNaturalist. Our system encourages collaborative recording throughout the year while preserving expert review where needed.

Problem Statement

How might we encourage citizens and visitors to collect more sighting data to improve their understanding and educate about their characteristics while maintaining efficient scientific records?

Hew Zhong Xuan

What I'm Proud Of

Creating user-centered prototypes that effectively communicated our vision to stakeholders. The iterative design process helped us refine concepts based on real feedback.

What I'm Not Proud Of

Designing for citizen science requires balancing simplicity with scientific rigor. Users need tools that are easy to use but still maintain data quality standards.

Alvis Ngo Kok Wei

What I'm Proud Of

Successfully researching and proposing the iNaturalist API integration strategy. Understanding the technical feasibility was crucial for creating a realistic implementation plan.

What I'm Not Proud Of

The importance of understanding existing platforms and APIs before designing new solutions. Leveraging established tools like iNaturalist can significantly accelerate development.

Ng Jun Long

What I'm Proud Of

Ong Ming Feng Benedict

What I'm Proud Of

Creating comprehensive documentation that effectively communicated our process and findings. The project website serves as a complete record of our journey.

Tan Kwan Yee

What I'm Proud Of

Conducting thorough user research that informed our design decisions. Understanding the needs of both experts and citizen scientists was crucial for our solution's success.

What I'm Not Proud Of
