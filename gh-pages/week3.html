Week 3: Presenting Poster and Finalizing Website Design

In this week, we will focus on creating a poster to present our project and finalizing the design of our website.

Final Week Idea

The Final Week of the Species Identification Project focuses on presenting the project through a poster and finalizing the website design. The goal is to effectively communicate the project's objectives, findings, and proposed solutions to a wider audience. The poster serves as a visual summary of the project, highlighting key aspects such as the problem statement, proposed solution, and the overall impact of the Species Identification Project. The website will be updated to include all the information gathered throughout the project, providing a comprehensive resource for anyone interested in the Species Identification Project.

Poster

To properly communicate the project and its goals, I created a poster that summarizes the key aspects of the Species Identification Project. The poster is designed to be visually appealing and informative, making it suitable for display at events or online sharing.
The poster includes the project description, problem statement, and proposed solution. This encapsulates the essence of the project and provides a clear overview for anyone interested in the Species Identification Project.

Poster designed to summarize the Species Identification Project.

Website

The website contains all the information that the team has gathered over the past weeks, including the project description, problem statement, proposed solution, and the poster. It serves as a comprehensive resource for anyone interested in the Species Identification Project. Through this, the team aims to provide a clear and accessible platform for sharing the project's goals and findings.

Summary

The final week of the Species Identification Project focuses on presenting the project through a poster and finalizing the website design. The poster serves as a visual summary of the project, highlighting key aspects such as the problem statement, proposed solution of the Species Identification Project. The website will be updated to include all the information gathered throughout the project, providing a comprehensive resource for anyone interested in the Species Identification Project.
